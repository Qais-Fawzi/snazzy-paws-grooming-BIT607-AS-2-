About Us

Snazzy Paws Grooming was founded by a couple with a lifelong passion for dogs and a commitment to providing top-notch grooming to the Hamilton community. We understand that bringing your dog in for grooming can be stressful — for both of you — so we focus on creating a calm, caring environment where every dog feels safe and comfortable.

Our experienced team has a deep knowledge of breed-specific grooming standards, and we take the time to get to know every dog and their individual needs. Whether your pup is a regular visitor or a first-timer, you can trust us to treat them like one of our own.
